Clean Future
TinoHacks
2nd Place, Best E-commerce Hack

The app determines if water is clean using Haar Wavelet Algorithm, uses the eBay API to buy water filtering products through SMS without internet, and uses the Google Maps API and custom database from prior app results to show nearby locations of clean water. We used the Twilio API to incorporate the messaging feature.

CalHacks
Fellowship Winner

This app is aimed at helping low-income women who lack resources to feminine hygiene products. Essentially, users can sign up as individuals either requesting or donating these feminine care products. Donators have access to a feed of requests; and can then complete the requests as they click on the specific one they want to complete. Requests are connected to the Postmates API, and a product can immediately be delivered to the requester.
