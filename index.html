Fellowship Winner

This app is aimed at helping low-income women who lack resources to feminine hygiene products. Essentially, users can sign up as individuals either requesting or donating these feminine care products. Donators have access to a feed of requests; and can then complete the requests as they click on the specific one they want to complete. Requests are connected to the Postmates API, and a product can immediately be delivered to the requester. Technologies used: Firebase, Postmates API, Xcode, Swift.
